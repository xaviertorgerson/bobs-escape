Common Questions

I already send invoices through Square or QuickBooks. Why do I need Handshake?

+

Think of QuickBooks as your accountant and Square as your cash register. Handshake is your digital storefront. A standard invoicing app lets you process a transaction, but Handshake provides the high-end digital proposal, the legally binding e-signatures, and the structured milestone payment schedule. It creates the premium client experience that makes a homeowner confident enough to hand over a $15,000 deposit in the first place.

Does Handshake hold my funds, or do I get paid directly?

+

We never touch your money. Handshake acts as a modern, automated alternative to construction escrow, but the funds route directly through your connected Square account. When a client taps "Approve" on a project phase, the payment is processed instantly via credit card or ACH and deposited into your bank account according to your normal processor schedule. No holding periods, no middleman wallets.

What if my clients aren't tech-savvy? Do they need to remember a password?

+

No, and this is our biggest advantage for winning residential bids. Requiring homeowners to download an app or create a login creates friction. Handshake uses a secure, encrypted web link sent directly via SMS text message or email. If your client knows how to open a text message, they can review the scope, sign the agreement, and pay the deposit natively in their phone's web browser in under 60 seconds.

I'm not a general contractor. Does phase-based billing work for my business?

+

Absolutely. While built to solve the deposit and cash flow problems of physical tradesmen‚Äîlike remodelers, landscapers, HVAC technicians, plumbers, and custom builders‚ÄîHandshake is the operating system for anyone who requires upfront agreements and milestone payouts. Software developers, creative agencies, interior designers, and high-ticket freelancers use Handshake to manage their own "Milestone Paywalls." If your work requires a proposal, a handshake, and payment protection, this tool is built for you.
