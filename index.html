Common Questions

Do my clients have to download an app?

+

No. This is our biggest advantage. ProLink uses a secure web interface accessed via a standard SMS text link. It removes all friction so you get paid faster.

How do I get the money into my bank account?

+

ProLink integrates directly with Square. You connect your Square account during setup. When a client pays a milestone, the money goes straight into your Square account, bypassing us entirely. Standard Square processing fees apply.

Will clients get my personal cell phone number?

+

If you are on the $39/mo Solo plan or higher, we provision a dedicated local business number for you through Twilio. Clients text that number, and it securely routes to the ProLink app, keeping your personal cell private.
